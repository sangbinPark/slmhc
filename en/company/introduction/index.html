About us

A company that communicates with customers based on trust and technology, Jungwon Electronics Co., Ltd., established in 2011, is a remanufacturing and export-oriented manufacturing company specializing in the production and sale of consumables such as office multifunction devices and PCUs. In particular, we are carrying out a factory that produces and supplies OEMs for carriers and developers, which are major core materials, under the technical support of Sindoh. As an export agency for items of each part of Sindoh, a global leader, we are expanding our capabilities by exporting genuine and remanufactured products of Ricoh and Konica Minolta to the world.

First, production and supply of developer and carrier for copier printers.

- In cooperation with Sindoh, the transfer of developer and carrier production facilities, and OEM production and supply contracts have been signed to establish the Asan Dunpo Plant and relocate the head office. (Production started at the end of 2021).

Second, PCU (Photo Conductor Unit)

- Ricoh Aficio produces and exports genuine PCU and remanufactured PCU.

Third, remanufactured PCU and remanufacturing unit for Konica Minolta

- Manufactures and sells various remanufacturing units such as transfer belt units, drum units, fixing units, toner cartridges, developing units, toners, rollers and sleeves for Konica Minolta multifunction printer.

Jungwon Electronics Co., Ltd. is constantly striving for technology development and quality control in order to always promise better quality to customers.
